DiffraNet: A Dataset of Serial Crystallography Diffraction Patterns

DiffraNet is a dataset with over 25,000 labeled serial crystallography diffraction images.

Dataset

DiffraNet is comprised of 512x512 grayscale images, divided into:

  • 25,000 synthetic images, labeled as blank, no-crystal, weak, good, or strong.
  • 457 real raw images, labeled as diffraction or no-diffraction.
  • 457 real preprocessed images, labeled as diffraction or no-diffraction.


Baseline models

Alongside DiffraNet, we provide a suite of baseline models in our GitHub repo:

  • GLCM, LBP, and SIFT+BoVW feature extractors.
  • RF and SVM classifiers.
  • Our novel CNN topology dubbed DeepFreak.
  • BOHB and Hyperopt AutoML optimization scripts.

For details on DiffraNet and our baselines models, see our DeepFreak paper.
